Autonomous Self-destructing Co-Military UAV

Project Idea

In the armed forces, specific units (battalions, platoons, etc.) are equipped with self-destructing UAVs and are engaged in their use.

If it is necessary to activate the device, the unit tries to get as close as possible to the hotline and activates it from a convenient location.

Those actions are not characterized as rapid response actions. In order to reduce the time to bring the weapon into combat position and put it into action, it would be advisable to have a co-military UAV "attached to the relevant troops of almost all military units", will be easily transportable, quick to operate, compact, easy to use, and self-managing.The proposed project solves the listed problems, as the above-mentioned model will be equipped with all the necessary features.

The primary goal

The goal of the project is to create a "co-military" Unmanned Aerial Vehicle and make this weapon available to the relevant troops of the Ministry of Defense of the Republic of Armenia, and to make the modern approach available to the military-industrial complexes in order to develop that branch in the military industry and find its application in the army

The device will be self-managing and self-destructing, fast and easily portable. The device destroys the target points if they are found in the system; otherwise, it continues looking for them by moving forward․ During defensive-offensive operations, the device can be used as a "small air rapid defense" on the front line or on a temporary newly opened front line.

Target problems.

• Simplify the use of the device as much as possible and make it safer, i.e. take the user out of the observed zone

• Make it applicable in case of surprise combat operations.

• Reduce the time to bring it into combat mode.

To achieve the goal, the actions should be performed in the following order:

1. Submit the project to the financing company -> get the necessary funds -> prepare all the prototypes -> do the test flights -> complete the software part -> get a patent for the idea.

2. Submit the project to the supervisory authorities -> get their written consent -> introduce the model to the manufacturing companies or through additional financing organize the production process independently.

Tasks:

To achieve the goal, the following tasks must be completed:

1. Determine what material and what technology should be used to make the device and transport box. Choose what brand and model of electronic systems to use.

2. Perform physical-mathematical calculations, prepare the first model with radio control, which will confirm the accuracy of physical-mathematical calculations, then prepare models equipped with software and optical devices.

3. Submit the project to the Ministries of High-Tech Industry and Defense, get approval.

Disturbing circumstances։

1. The funding, which will be overcome through the grant.

2. Getting approval from the Ministry of Defense and the Ministry of High-Tech Industry, which we will overcome by correctly presenting the negative and positive aspects of the product and showing all its capabilities.

What problem will be solved by the proposed model?

Self-destructing small drones must also be controlled from the line of contact by the person operating the drone (military). It is also possible to "disrupt the connection", that is, to disable the radio communication link by means of radio electronic warfare systems. Also, it is more expensive and time-consuming to train operators from contract and conscript soldiers.

To avoid all this, the proposed model will be self-guided (it will search for different types of targets), handheld, which is more effective in terms of firing from different positions and quick response, and is less expensive. To make the transportation of drones faster, easier and safer, it will be placed in a container.

The innovative mind։

The companies operating in the Republic of Armenia do not have a similar product, their self-destructing UAVs do not have all these features at the same time. Considering these circumstances, we can say that this model will be an innovation in the Armenian military industry.
